The purpose of this app is to allow users to keep track of any vacations they are planning. It allows users to add vacations manually including dates and hotels, as well as excursions that they are planning to go on during their vacations. The app allows users to set alerts for these upcoming events, as well as share the information with others using their mobile devices. The app also allows users to download an easy-to-read PDF file with all of their inputted information which they can print out so they can have a physical planner as well as a digital one.
